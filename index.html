Welcome to Virtual Donation Box

Virtual Donation Box is an online platform that brings together donors and donees to make it easy and meaningful to donate essential items. Donors can offer items like clothes, books, groceries, and other daily essentials by this platform. After sharing details such as their address, type of donation, and available time for pickup, their contribution is listed for those in need. Donations can come from households, college messes, canteens, events, hotels, or any institution willing to give. Donees are underprivileged individuals and communities in need of basic items. Their portal is accessed and managed by NGOs and welfare organizations, who collect the donated goods and ensure they are delivered to the right people.
